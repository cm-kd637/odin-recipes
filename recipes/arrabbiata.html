Arrabbiata

How to make the best Arrabiata pasta

This recipe will blow your socks off. It's hands down one of my favourite pasta receipes and, best of all, it's quick, easy and cheap to make. And, you won't need to read about my life story to find out how!

Ingredients

  • 400g/ 14oz penne or ziti , or other pasta of choice (short or long)
  • 1 tbsp cooking / kosher salt , for cooking pasta

    • Arrabiata sauce:

  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves , finely minced
  • 1 red cayenne pepper chilli , finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes (red pepper flakes)
  • 800g / 28 oz canned crushed tomato
  • 1 1/4 tsp cooking / kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

    • Serving:

  • parmesan , finely grated
  • 2 tsp finely chopped parsley (optional)

    Steps

  1. Boil your pasta for 10-12 minutes
  2. Once the pasta is on, chuck your garlic in a frying pan and cook for 2 minutes, until fragrant
  3. Add tomatoes, chillis, salt a pepper
  4. Watch in utter astonishment as magic occurs
  5. Mix it all together, hoping for the best. And yes, remove the pasta water first
  6. Eat and marvel at your culinary prowess.
