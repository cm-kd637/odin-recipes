Lasagne

How to make the best Lasagne

This lasagne recipe is a game-changer. It’s one of my all-time favorite pasta dishes, and the best part? It’s simple, affordable, and packed with flavor. No long backstory, just delicious lasagne made easy!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets

    • Meat Sauce:

  • 500g / 1 lb ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g / 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

    • Cheese Mixture:

  • 250g / 9 oz ricotta cheese
  • 250g / 9 oz mozzarella, shredded
  • 50g / 1.7 oz parmesan, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

    • Other:

  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella, shredded (for topping)

    Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) because warm homes make happy lasagne.
  2. Brown your beef in a pan until it’s looking deliciously golden, then toss in some onions and garlic. Cook until fragrant (about 3-4 minutes).
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and a hint of basil. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, letting all those flavors become best friends.
  4. In another bowl, mix ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan. This is the cheesy dream team. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Take your lasagne dish and start layering: a bit of meat sauce, a layer of pasta sheets, then cheese mix. Repeat like a culinary architect until you run out.
  6. Top with extra mozzarella, because why not go all out?
  7. Slide it into the oven for 40-45 minutes. Let the magic happen.
  8. Once golden and bubbly, pull it out. Let it rest for 10 minutes (if you can resist).
  9. Serve, enjoy, and prepare for the praise that's about to come your way.
