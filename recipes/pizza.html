Pizza

How to make the best Traditional Italian Pizza

This pizza recipe is as close to the authentic taste of Italy as you can get without booking a flight! It's simple, delicious, and will leave you craving more. And guess what? No need for a long backstory to enjoy this masterpiece.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 500g / 3 1/4 cups Tipo "00" flour (or all-purpose flour)
  • 325ml / 1 1/3 cups lukewarm water
  • 10g / 2 tsp salt
  • 7g / 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, to help yeast activate)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Pizza Sauce:

  • 400g / 14 oz canned San Marzano tomatoes, crushed
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for added flavor)

Toppings:

  • 200g / 7 oz fresh mozzarella, torn into small pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Drizzle of extra virgin olive oil (for finishing)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the lukewarm water, yeast, and sugar (if using). Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a separate large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Gradually add the yeast mixture and olive oil, stirring until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place it in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  4. Meanwhile, make the sauce. In a small saucepan, heat the olive oil and sauté the garlic for 1-2 minutes. Add the crushed tomatoes, salt, and oregano. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then set aside to cool.
  5. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (usually around 250°C / 480°F) and, if you have one, place a pizza stone inside to heat up.
  6. Once the dough has risen, divide it into 2 or 4 portions, depending on the size of pizzas you want. Roll each portion out on a floured surface into a thin circle.
  7. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the base of each pizza, leaving a small border for the crust. Add torn mozzarella pieces evenly over the top.
  8. Transfer the pizzas to a hot pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 7-10 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. Slice, serve, and enjoy the pizza that’ll transport you straight to Italy!
